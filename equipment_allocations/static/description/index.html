Equipment Allocations

Since allocation of equipment includes a process of multiple stages, therefore, it is very important for an organization to manage its resources. With the help of our module, Odoo Equipment Allocations employees can easily request for allocation of equipment. Also, allocation request mail will be sent to followers of that resource category and maintenance team.

Basic Features
  • Using this app employee can easily request for equipment allocation.
  • Introduce new group named 'Equipment' in Equipment Allocations.
  • Equipments can be linked to the product.
  • Employees can request for equipment as 'on-demand' or 'permanent'.
  • Restrict user to allocate the same equipment to multiple requests.
  • Allocation request mail will be sent to the followers of the category and maintenance team.
  • Equipment allocation request approval mail will be sent to the Requester of the request.
  • Equipment user can cancel the allocation request of the employee.
  • Manage purchase date and warranty period of the equipment.
  • Permanently allocated equipment can be replaced at any time.
